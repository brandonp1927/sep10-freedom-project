Drills

Drills are handheld devices, the first electric drill was first invented in 1889 by Arthur James Arnot and William Blanch Brain at Melbourne Australia who had the i4dea to combine it to the electric motor. Then someone had the idea to design the drill to fit in the user's hand and make it portable and those were Wilhelm and Carl Fein in Germany. The drill is a tool that uses rotation to make a hole into metal, wood, to also tighten screws, loosen screws. There are 16 different types of drills that are similar but have many different purposes. But here are the main 3 which are traditional drills that are used for basic house projects, drilling holes, and drilling needles into wood. Impact Driver is for more heavy work, they drill fasteners into place, drilling in long screws with rapid pounding movements. Hammer drills drill quickly into concrete, and are used for demolition.
